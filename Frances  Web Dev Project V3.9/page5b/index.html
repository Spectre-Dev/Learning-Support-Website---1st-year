The principal role of Student Support is to coordinate, enhance and introduce support services to ensure that students have a rewarding and fulfilling experience, both personally and academically, while at college. The Student Support Office is guided by the Wellness Concept which values a students social, physical, psychological and spiritual wellbeing.
